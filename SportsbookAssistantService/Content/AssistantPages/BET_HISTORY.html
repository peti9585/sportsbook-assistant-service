Bet History

Review all your past bets, settled and pending, in one convenient location.

What You Can Do

  • View settled bets with outcomes
  • Check pending bets and cash out options
  • Filter by date range and bet type
  • Export bet history for your records

Understanding Bet Status

Pending: Bet is active and awaiting results.
Won: Your bet was successful.
Lost: Your bet was unsuccessful.
Void: Bet was cancelled (stake returned).
