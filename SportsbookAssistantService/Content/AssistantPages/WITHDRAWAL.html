Withdraw Funds

Request a withdrawal of your available balance using your preferred method.

Withdrawal Methods

  • Bank transfer (1-3 business days)
  • E-wallets (24-48 hours)
  • Credit/Debit card (3-5 business days)

Important Information

Withdrawals are processed to the same method used for deposit where possible. First-time withdrawals may require identity verification. Minimum and maximum withdrawal limits apply.
